# Dashboard Plan: Nonprofit Grant Reporting Data System

## Dashboard Purpose

This dashboard is designed to help nonprofit organizations monitor grant funding, spending progress, reporting status, compliance issues, and beneficiary reach.

The dashboard will support donor reporting, grant management, compliance monitoring, and program performance tracking.

---

## Dashboard Users

The intended users of this dashboard include:

- Grant managers
- Monitoring and evaluation specialists
- Donor reporting teams
- Nonprofit leadership
- Finance and operations teams
- Program managers
- Data analysts

---

## Key Performance Indicators

The dashboard may include the following KPI cards:

- Total grant amount
- Total amount spent
- Remaining grant balance
- Overall spending rate
- Total beneficiaries reached
- Number of active grants
- Number of grants with compliance issues
- Number of delayed or incomplete reports

---

## Planned Dashboard Sections

### 1. Executive Summary

This section will provide a high-level overview of grant funding, spending progress, beneficiaries reached, and reporting risks.

### 2. Donor Funding Summary

This section will show total grant amounts by donor and help identify which donors provide the largest funding amounts.

### 3. Program Area Spending

This section will summarize spending by program area, including health, education, workforce development, economic development, and community services.

### 4. Spending Rate Analysis

This section will compare grant amounts and amount spent to calculate spending rates and identify grants that may need follow-up.

### 5. Reporting Status Monitoring

This section will summarize reporting status categories such as submitted, delayed, incomplete, or under review.

### 6. Compliance Monitoring

This section will identify grants with compliance issues that may require documentation, review, or management follow-up.

### 7. Beneficiary Reach

This section will show how many beneficiaries were reached by program area, donor, and region.

### 8. Regional Grant Performance

This section will summarize grant amount, spending, and beneficiaries reached by region.

## Suggested Dashboard Filters

The dashboard may include filters for:

- Donor
- Program area
- Region
- Reporting period
- Reporting status
- Compliance issue
- Spending category

---

## Expected Dashboard Insights

The dashboard is expected to help answer questions such as:

1. Which donors provide the largest grant amounts?
2. Which program areas have the highest spending?
3. Which grants have low spending rates?
4. Which grants have delayed or incomplete reporting?
5. Which grants have compliance issues?
6. Which program areas reached the most beneficiaries?
7. Which regions show stronger grant performance?
8. Which grants require management follow-up?
